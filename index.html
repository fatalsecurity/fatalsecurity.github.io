About Us



Fatal Security is a security consulting firm based out of Atlanta, Georgia. We offer a variety of Information Security solutions and services. The core of our business is to offer in-depth penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, and social engineering assessments.


We are a seasoned team of Information Security professionals, incredibly passionate about our field. Our highly skilled team of engineers excel in vulnerability reconnaissance, exploitation, and remediation. Each team member hold at minimum a technical Bachelor's degree and actively participate in the open-source security community. We boast over 10 years of direct, hands on experience, and hold numerous industry standard security certifications:

  • ISC2 Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)
  • SANS GIAC Penetration Tester (GPEN)
  • EC-Council Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)
  • Offensive Security Certified Penetration Tester (OSCP)
  • Comptia Security+
